Jett

An agile fighter who gets around the battlefield quickly to surprise her enemies. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies up before they even know what hit them.

Raze

A fiery damage dealer who excels at flushing enemies out of entrenched positions. Her explosive arsenal threatens to level the playing field.

Viper

A powerful controller who can manipulate the battlefield with her poisonous chemicals. If the toxins don't kill her enemies, her mind games surely will.
